Other Third Place websites

Greatgoodplace.org is a website created by Karen Christenen to demonstrate the importance of third places. Karen worked with Ray Oldenberg to publish the book "The Great Good Place" The book is known for coining the term "Third place"

Project for Public Spaces is a non-profit that creates public spaces by collaborating with community members. They showcase examples of great third places in the following categories.

  • Small quickly created third places designed to develop norms of community driven placemaking
  • Urban districts with great public spaces
  • Public buildings
  • Public squares
  • Parks
