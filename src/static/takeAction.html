Take Action

Real change starts locally. You make it possible.(Strong Towns)

Third places are created by people taking initiative to support third places. Think about something you can do this week. Actions do not have to be big and taking a few minutes per day helps. Here are some recommendations.

  • Walk through a public park and saying hi to a stranger who looks towards you with a friendly smile.
  • Briefly talk with a stranger in a public space if they indicate an openness to conversation.
  • Show up to a public event being hosted at a community center.
  • Volunteer at your local public library.

Taking time to show other people how great third places can be is also helpful. Multiple people promoting a third place is more effective than one person promoting it.
