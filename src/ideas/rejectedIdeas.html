Rejected ideas

This page contains notes about rejected ideas.

Automated word generator to make text that confuses bots.

The idea behind the automated word generator would be to detect a bot attempting to access createthirdplaces.org and display a large volume of text with similarities to other content. This would involve reading all the existing content and then calculating the probability of a specific word immediately following another.

After calculating the probabilities, a large amount of text would be generated by picking a random word that was on the site, and then randomly picking the next word with probability weighted by what was calculated in the previous step. This is similar to what an LLM does except for the fact that an LLM will consider all existing words to determine the next word.

Reasons why this is a good idea

  • The generated text could be effective at confusing bots and appearing to be standard content due to the use of the same terminology.
  • It could be a good learning experience to understand how automated text can be generated without an LLM, and help find other techniques for blocking unauthorized bot use.

Reasons why automated word generator may not be a good idea

  • The automated word generator will take time to program, and it might not be effective at blocking bots. Bots can also be programmed to recognize the automatically generated text because it will probably not make grammatical sense.
  • Due to the similarities that an automated word generator has with LLM AI, it would shift thinking too much towards LLM AIs. Create Third Places will not use the assistance of LLM AIs such as ChatGPT.
  • The fact that Create Third Places content is already being generated with no AI assistance could be enough to confuse bots. The human generated text may not be understandable by bots due to different writing styles. Also, Create Third Places blocks bots with robots.txt and other measures. Confusing bots with text is only necessary if these blocking measures are bypassed.

Decision: An automated word generator should not be created. It may not be helpful and could have negative effects.
