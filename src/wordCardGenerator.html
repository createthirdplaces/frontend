Word card generator

This is a fanmade expansion for the Just One game. Click the print option in your web browser, and a printable version will automatically be generated.

Expansion overview

The expansion is split up into multiple modules which can be played individually or combined.

Module 1: Traitor

One person will be designated as the traitor and is trying to get rid of clues. They will take two Just One easels and write one word on each one in a round. If any of the cluegivers write a word that is identical to the traitor, their clue is hidden. After comparing easels, the traitor's words will be hidden.

A traitor will score 1 point for each word that is not correctly guessed.

Module 2: Extra cards

These are extra word cards where more difficult words have higher numbers.
